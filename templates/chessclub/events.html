Weekly Events

We meet weekly on Fridays, play casual blitz, and cater food (usually pizza). We also occasionally host unrated blitz and rapid cash prize tournaments. Subscribe to our mailing-list to know the room we are meeting in and the theme of the meeting.

Annual Events

We usually host 2 USCF rated regular tournaments: one in the fall and one in the spring. If finances permit, we also attend the Pan-American Collegiate and US Amateur Team East. Subscribe to our mailing-list to know dates, location, and how to register.

Fall Open

A rated USCF tournament usually hosted in early November on MIT Campus. Check back here for exact date, location, and schedule.

Spring Open

A rated USCF tournament usually hosted in early May on MIT Campus. Check back here for exact date, location, and schedule.
